ABOUT

The main motive of this website is to provide a reliable service to our users with guaranteed privacy. This website holds the details of the patient to book their appointment with the doctors and can view their previous and upcoming appointments, prescriptions, reports etc.

Manakamana Hospital (MH) is a well equipped multi-speciality hospital located in Chhebetar, Gorkha. It is led by a team of compassionate multi-disciplinary, highly trained doctors, nurses and technicians who can provide the best care to every patient through integrated clinical service and education.

    Our Mission

Our mission is the improvement of health and the alleviation of pain, suffering and sickness for the people we serve. We aim to be the number one choice for patients, doctors, medical practitioners and employees.
